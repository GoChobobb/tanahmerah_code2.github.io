TIMOR COTTAGES

The two Timor Cottages at Tanah Merah Art Resort originate from the island of Timor in Eastern Indonesia and are beautifully decorated with traditional designs by master woodcarvers. Each of the raised cottages accommodates you with a cozy room, a modern bathroom with shower and bathtub as well as a spacious veranda for relaxing and enjoying the lush surroundings. Set in peaceful tropical gardens close to the infinity pool and restaurant, our Timor Cottages offer superb value with their unique ambiance and spectacular rainforest views. Both cottages are fit for two persons.
