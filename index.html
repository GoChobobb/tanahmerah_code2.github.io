Perched high above the magnificent Petanu river gorge with breathtaking tropical rainforest views and private access to the river and waterfall, Tanah Merah Art Resort offers an unmatched experience of artistic indulgence and blissful tranquility.

This enchanting art resort features 31 artistically designed villas and luxury studios which have been dressed in the warm colors of the genuine Bali and lovingly decorated with exquisite Indonesian art, antiques, and furniture.

Our trademark is the unique Tanah Merah Collection and Gallery which displays an unsurpassed private collection of oriental paintings, antiques and textiles, celebrating the fascinating history of Indonesia going back 2000 years.

The busy and artistic town of Ubud is just a ten-minute drive away on one of Bali’s most scenic routes with panoramic views of the iconic rice terraces and majestic volcanos.
